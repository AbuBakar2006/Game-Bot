# Bot V2
* In this version I decided to not detect any foods and only focus on the monster and shredder.
* The Bot will also not copy the screen on the laptop window and only shows the results in the terminal.
* For food collection the bot will keep swiping left and right on screen.
* When a monster is detected the bot will stop swiping and focus on moving shredder to the opposite side of the monster.
* When the monster is past the shredder, The bot will continue swiping.

## What is Achived
* FPS increased from 1-3 to 14-17.
* No need to take screenshots for the bot to work.

## Future Adjustments
